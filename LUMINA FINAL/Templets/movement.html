🏃‍♀️ Movement & Mood

Discover how physical activity can enhance your mental well-being and mood.

🌞 Start with Light Activity

A short walk, gentle yoga, or stretching can help release endorphins.

⏰ Stay Consistent

Even 10–20 minutes a day of movement can make a difference.

🌿 Connect with Nature

Exercising outdoors helps reduce anxiety and stress.

🧘‍♂️ Mindful Movement

Try activities like tai chi or dancing. Focus on your breath and body.

👯 Move Together

Working out with others boosts motivation and social connection.
