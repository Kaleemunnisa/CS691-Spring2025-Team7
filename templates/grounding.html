Reconnect with the present moment during stress or anxiety. Here are simple, powerful ways to center yourself:

5-4-3-2-1 Technique

Notice 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, and 1 you can taste. Feel your senses guide you back to now.

Deep Breathing

Inhale slowly for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts, and exhale for 6 counts. Repeat this cycle to calm your mind and body.

Hold a Comforting Object

Find something soft, smooth, or textured. Focus your attention entirely on how it feels in your hands.

Feet on the Ground

Plant your feet firmly. Wiggle your toes. Feel the solid support of the earth beneath you.
